NUTS conversion matrices

- Creators: Cristian Pigaiani and Filipe Batista (JRC.B.3)
- Version: August 2022
- Available covariates for variable conversion between NUTS versions:
    areaKm: Surface in sq. km (source: EuroBoundaryMaps, EuroGeographics)
    pop18: Resident population in 2018 (JRC-GEOSTAT, Joint Research Centre)
    pop11: Resident population in 2011 (GEOSTAT, Eurostat)
    artif_surf18: Artificial surfaces in 2018 (LUISA Base Map 2012, Joint Research Centre);
    artif_surf12: Artificial surfaces in 2012 (LUISA Base Map 2012, Joint Research Centre);
- More info: https://urban.jrc.ec.europa.eu/nutsconverter
